Quick Start

1. Go to the Creator tab to start writing. Your work is saved to your browser automatically.

2. Go to the Library tab to read "Featured Stories" (from your GitHub `stories/` folder) and your own "Local Creations."

3. Go to the Settings tab to export your creations. To publish, you must manually add the exported `.json` file to your GitHub `stories/` folder.
