The Chronicle of the Void Sovereign

Chapter 1: The Rat in the Red Snow

Future Text-to-Speech (TTS) Player Here

The world of Zhenha runs on World Energy, or Qi. It is the "RAM" of the earth. Cultivators "sync" with it, storing power in their Dantians. Their rank, from a 9th Rate Novice to a 1st Rate Master, is their "allocated" power.

Mael, age 5, was an unallocated 0.00. He was a rat, a ghost in the frozen alleys of Iron-Peak City. His world was the gnawing cold in his belly and the hatred he felt for the laughing, well-fed merchants who kicked him.

His world *ended* the night he witnessed the massacre of the Silver-Leaf House. Hidden in a trash heap, he saw assassins from the "Shadow Scorpion" sect move like ghosts, their green Qi-blades silencing an entire bloodline. He saw a child, his own age, about to be cut down. He did the only thing he could: he screamed. A desperate, childish cry for help.

The sound echoed. The assassins froze. A figure appeared instantly before Mael, his aura so intense it melted the snow. He was the 1st Rate Master, the Sect Leader. His eyes were cold, bored, and disgusted. "A rat."
